# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone
- Do not click on unknown links or messages
- Verify payment requests before accepting
- Use strong passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for transactions
- Regularly check your bank statements
- Install apps only from trusted sources

## UPI Safety Tips
- Always check the receiver’s name before sending money
- Do not approve unknown collect/payment requests

## Report Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
